Countries & Capitals Countries

Usage

  • Click on "Countries & Capitals" in the upper left to go to the home page.

  • Click on the "Browse Countries" button to list all countries in the world.

    • Hover over a country in the left panel to display the capital, land area and population. The population and area are displayed in an infograph. The largest bubbles represents the country with the largest population or area.

    • Click on a country to display even more information (Capital, Flag, Map, Country Code, Neighboring Countries, Timezone, Continent, ...)

  • When viewing a country and its capital, click on one of the neighboring countries link to view its neighbor.
