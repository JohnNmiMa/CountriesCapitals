Countries & Capitals

Usage

  • Click on "Browse Countries" to list all countries in the world

  • Hover over a country in the left panel to display the capital, land area and population

  • Click on a country to display even more information (Flag, Country Code, Neighboring Countries, ...)

  • Click on "Countries & Capitals" in the upper left to go to the home page
